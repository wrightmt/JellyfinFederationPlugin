📁 Next Step: Add Libraries in Jellyfin

After syncing, follow these steps to make content visible:

  1. Go to Dashboard → Libraries → Add Media Library
  2. Select content type (Movies or TV Shows)
  3. Click "Add folder" and enter: /config/data/federation/[Mapping Name]
  4. Click "OK" and wait for scan to complete
  5. View your content in the new library! 🎉

Note: This is a one-time setup per mapping. Future syncs will automatically update the library.
